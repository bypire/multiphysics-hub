Scope: this solver is Stam "Stable Fluids" on a collocated grid — fast and stable, but its projection leaves a small residual divergence (collocated odd–even decoupling), so the field is qualitative rather than a quantitative incompressible solve. A staggered-grid (MAC) Navier–Stokes solver validated against Ghia/Williamson lives in the separate vortex-street-cfd project. The drag and downforce used by the car come from the coefficient model (½ρC·V²).
